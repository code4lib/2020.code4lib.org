The Newcomer Dinner is an opportunity for veterans of the code4lib conference to meet and welcome first-timers. Attendees sign up to join others for dinner the night before the main sessions of conference begin. {% if site.data.conf.newcomer-dinner.signup-url != '' %} See information about the dinner and the sign-up list here. Attendees are responsible in assessing the accessibility of the restaurant space of their choice. {% endif %}

Transportation

Attendees must arrange for their own transportation to the restaurants. {% if site.data.conf.newcomer-dinner.activities-info != '' %} To get a sense of the locations and neighborhoods, you can check out the Activities Map. {% endif %}

For restaurants that are not close to the public transportation stops, we recommend carpooling with a local, calling a taxi, or using a ride share service. {% if site.data.conf.newcomer-dinner.transit-info != '' %} Here are some public transit options for the various neighborhoods noted in the restaurant list. {% endif %}
